About Us

Work Hub MadeSimple is a flexible and friendly co-working space in the heart of London’s tech scene located near Old Street roundabout.

Over 10 years' experience in company formations and the startup industry has taught us that budget constraints make office space a problem for many new businesses. Through Work Hub MadeSimple, we are able to help companies grow and continue our relationships with our valued customers. Our aim is to provide a welcoming and supportive environment for startups. We offer a choice of regular desks, stand-up desks, comfy seating and meeting rooms to suit your requirements
